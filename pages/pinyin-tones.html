Chinese Tones (声调)

Mandarin Chinese is a tonal language with four main tones and one neutral tone. The tone of a syllable can change its meaning completely. Click on each example to hear the pronunciation.

First Tone (ˉ)
妈
mā - mother
High and level tone
Second Tone (ˊ)
麻
má - hemp
Rising tone
Third Tone (ˇ)
马
mǎ - horse
Falling-rising tone
Fourth Tone (ˋ)
骂
mà - scold
Falling tone
