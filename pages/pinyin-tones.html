声调 - Pinyin Tones

Learn the four tones and neutral tone in Mandarin Chinese.

Overview

Mandarin Chinese is a tonal language, meaning that the same syllable pronounced with different tones can have completely different meanings. There are four main tones and one neutral tone.

First Tone (ˉ)

High level tone

mā
妈 (mother)
yī
一 (one)

Second Tone (ˊ)

Rising tone

má
麻 (hemp)
yí
移 (move)

Third Tone (ˇ)

Falling-rising tone

mǎ
马 (horse)
yǐ
已 (already)

Fourth Tone (ˋ)

Falling tone

mà
骂 (scold)
yì
义 (justice)

Neutral Tone

Light and short tone

ma
吗 (question particle)
le
了 (completion particle)
