声母 - Pinyin Initials

Learn the consonant sounds that begin syllables in Mandarin Chinese.

Overview

Pinyin initials are the consonant sounds that can appear at the beginning of a syllable in Mandarin Chinese. There are 21 initials in total, grouped by their pronunciation characteristics.

Labial Sounds

b
like 'b' in "boy"
p
like 'p' in "spy"
m
like 'm' in "mom"
f
like 'f' in "fun"

Dental Sounds

d
like 'd' in "do"
t
like 't' in "stop"
n
like 'n' in "no"
l
like 'l' in "love"

Guttural Sounds

g
like 'g' in "go"
k
like 'k' in "sky"
h
like 'h' in "hot"
