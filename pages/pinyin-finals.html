韵母 - Pinyin Finals

Learn the vowel sounds that form the core of Chinese syllables.

Overview

Finals are the vowel sounds in Mandarin Chinese. They can be simple vowels, compound vowels, or vowels followed by n or ng. Click on each final to hear its pronunciation.

Simple Finals

a
like 'a' in "father"
o
like 'o' in "or"
e
like 'e' in "her"
i
like 'ee' in "see"
u
like 'oo' in "too"
ü
like German 'ü'

Compound Finals

ai
like 'y' in "my"
ei
like 'ay' in "say"
ao
like 'ow' in "now"
ou
like 'o' in "go"

Nasal Finals

an
like 'an' in "fan"
en
like 'un' in "fun"
ang
like 'ung' in "sung"
eng
like 'ung' in "hung"
