Basic Chinese Sentence Patterns

Learn these fundamental sentence patterns to start forming basic Chinese sentences. Click on any example to hear its pronunciation.

Subject + Verb

我吃饭

Wǒ chī fàn

I eat (rice/food)

Structure: Subject (我) + Verb (吃) + Object (饭)

Subject + 是 + Noun

我是学生

Wǒ shì xuéshēng

I am a student

Structure: Subject (我) + 是 + Noun (学生)

Subject + Adjective

天气很好

Tiānqì hěn hǎo

The weather is good

Structure: Subject (天气) + 很 + Adjective (好)

Question Pattern with 吗

你好吗

Nǐ hǎo ma

How are you?

Structure: Statement + 吗

Negative Pattern with 不

我不喜欢

Wǒ bù xǐhuān

I don't like

Structure: Subject + 不 + Verb
