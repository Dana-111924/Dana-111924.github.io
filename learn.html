Beginner's Guide (For Absolute Beginners)

Welcome to your first step in learning Chinese! Let's start with a basic greeting:

nǐ hǎo
你好
hello

This simple greeting consists of:

  • Pinyin: How we write the pronunciation using our alphabet
  • Characters: The actual Chinese writing

拼音 - Pinyin System

Pinyin is the official romanization system for Standard Chinese. It helps you learn pronunciation.

Basic Rules

Tones

  • mā (First tone - high and level)
    妈
    mother
  • má (Second tone - rising)
    麻
    hemp
  • mǎ (Third tone - falling-rising)
    马
    horse
  • mà (Fourth tone - falling)
    骂
    scold

句型 - Sentence Patterns

Basic Patterns

Question Formation

对话 - Conversations

Daily Conversations

Greeting

A: Nǐ hǎo!
A: 你好！
A: Hello!
B: Nǐ hǎo! Nǐ hǎo ma?
B: 你好！你好吗？
B: Hello! How are you?
A: Wǒ hěn hǎo, xièxie.
A: 我很好，谢谢。
A: I'm very well, thank you.

阅读练习 - Reading Practice

Simple Story

Zhè shì wǒ de jiā.
这是我的家。
This is my home.
Wǒ hé wǒ de jiārén zhù zài zhèlǐ.
我和我的家人住在这里。
I live here with my family.

学习建议 - Practice Tips

  • • Practice pronunciation with native speakers or language apps
  • • Write characters repeatedly to memorize them
  • • Listen to Chinese music and watch Chinese movies
  • • Use flashcards for vocabulary practice
