Beginner's Guide (For Absolute Beginners)

Welcome to your first step in learning Chinese! Let's start by understanding what Chinese characters look like.

nǐ hǎo
---This is Pinyin: How we write the pronunciation using our alphabet
你好
---These are the actual Chinese writing
hello
---Its meaning

"你好" looks like this in Chinese characters. When you learn Chinese, you'll see characters like this instead of letters. Pinyin helps with pronunciation and memory, but real Chinese is entirely made up of characters.

学习中文不仅是一种语言技能的提升，更是一场文化的探索之旅。在这门语言中，"你"代表着人与人之间的连接，而"好"则象征着美好的祝愿和正面的表达。

当你刚开始学习中文时，最常见的一个词就是"你好"。它不仅是打招呼的方式，也反映了中国文化中礼貌和友好的交流方式。而在更深入的学习中，你会发现"你好"中的每个字都有自己的含义，比如"你"表示对方，"好"表示美好、健康。当你能熟练运用这些词汇时，你就能够自信地和别人打招呼、表达自己的想法。

拼音 - Pinyin System

Pinyin is the official romanization system for Standard Chinese. It helps you learn pronunciation.

Basic Rules

Tones

  • mā (First tone - high and level)
    妈
    mother
  • má (Second tone - rising)
    麻
    hemp
  • mǎ (Third tone - falling-rising)
    马
    horse
  • mà (Fourth tone - falling)
    骂
    scold

Basic Patterns

Question Formation

对话 - Conversations

Daily Conversations

Greeting

A: Nǐ hǎo!
A: 你好！
A: Hello!
B: Nǐ hǎo! Nǐ hǎo ma?
B: 你好！你好吗？
B: Hello! How are you?
A: Wǒ hěn hǎo, xièxie.
A: 我很好，谢谢。
A: I'm very well, thank you.

阅读练习 - Reading Practice

Simple Story

Zhè shì wǒ de jiā.
这是我的家。
This is my home.
Wǒ hé wǒ de jiārén zhù zài zhèlǐ.
我和我的家人住在这里。
I live here with my family.

学习建议 - Practice Tips

  • • Practice pronunciation with native speakers or language apps
  • • Write characters repeatedly to memorize them
  • • Listen to Chinese music and watch Chinese movies
  • • Use flashcards for vocabulary practice
